About Us

Company Profile

JM Solution IT Services is an IT solutions provider serving businesses across Cebu with reliable, modern, and efficient technology solutions. The journey began in 2018 when our founder started providing freelance IT services to local businesses, focusing on dependable technical support, network solutions, and customer-focused service. As trust grew and more companies relied on the quality of work delivered, that freelance foundation evolved into a broader vision.

That growth led to the establishment of JM Solution IT Services, built on professionalism, reliability, and customer satisfaction. Today, we provide CCTV installation, structured cabling, network infrastructure, server solutions, fiber optic installation, telecommunications, IT support, and web development. Every solution is tailored to each client's unique needs, helping businesses strengthen security, improve productivity, and operate more confidently with responsive local support.
