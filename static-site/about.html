About Us

About JM Solution IT Services

JM Solution IT Services was built from a simple goal: help local businesses grow through reliable, modern, and efficient technology solutions. The journey began in 2018 when our founder started providing freelance IT services to businesses in Cebu, focusing on dependable technical support, network solutions, and customer-focused service. As demand grew and more clients placed their trust in the quality of work delivered, the vision expanded beyond freelance services.

That momentum led to the establishment of JM Solution IT Services, founded on professionalism, reliability, and customer satisfaction. Today, we support businesses across Cebu with CCTV installation, structured cabling, network infrastructure, server solutions, fiber optic installation, telecommunications, IT support, and web development. By combining technical expertise with a tailored, client-first approach, we help organizations improve productivity, strengthen security, and build a more dependable technology foundation for long-term success.
